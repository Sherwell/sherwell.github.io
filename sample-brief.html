What a ZeroToBuild Brief Looks Like

This isn't what the founder typed. It's the result of a conversation where ZeroToBuild helped them refine, challenge, and clarify their thinking until this brief emerged.

1 🧠 Define the Idea

Prompt: "Describe your idea so another founder says 'hell yes.'"

I'm building an AI co-pilot for overwhelmed Etsy sellers who spend hours replying to DMs and planning content. It helps them draft customer replies and generate shop-based content ideas.

The insight came from 3 shop owners who said: "I spend more time answering questions than creating."

Maturity Check:

  • ✅ Clear Target User
  • ✅ Specific Pain Point
  • ✅ Grounded in Real Insight

2 🎯 Define the MVP

Prompt: "What's the smallest version of your idea that still tests your core belief?"

A landing page and demo that shows how the co-pilot drafts replies to common Etsy questions. I'll manually simulate responses with GPT and test whether sellers trust the AI-generated replies.

Maturity Check:

  • ✅ Focused Scope
  • ✅ Tests the Core Risk
  • ✅ Doesn't Overbuild

3 🧪 Suggested Validation Plan

Core risk: Will Etsy sellers trust AI to talk to their customers?

ZeroToBuild might suggest:

  • ✅ Create a landing page showing example replies
  • ✅ Use GPT + Airtable to manually simulate the co-pilot
  • ✅ DM 5 Etsy sellers with a demo link and track reactions
  • ✅ Use built-in templates for landing pages and outreach

Want help building a landing page or messaging test? ZeroToBuild can generate those too.

4 🧠 Expert Feedback on Your Solution

After defining your idea and MVP, ZeroToBuild brings in AI-generated expert feedback—just like you'd get from a sharp product advisor or strategic cofounder.

🔍 Excerpt from AI Feedback:

"This MVP is a strong first test of trust, but the core risk is emotional, not technical. If Etsy sellers fear losing control, even a great AI response won't be enough. Your next step should include a trust-building UI pattern: draft-first, approve-before-send."

Feedback continues with suggestions around onboarding, wedge positioning, and messaging clarity.

💡 Sample Suggestions:

  • ✅ Add a visual confirmation step before AI replies are sent
  • ✅ Use a "human-approved" badge to build trust with buyers
  • ✅ Narrow early focus to Etsy shops with 100+ DMs/month for clearer pull signal

…and more, depending on your unique idea and MVP.
